State-egpeni nuugsimakuvek

Nutaghsaghqaqan nakmikistem ilatellgha neghraghaghpenun. Naalkigu state-en nalighmeng territory-nkanaghqughqi allangughtelleghten state-egpek ilatellghan tuunviga kaatfagilgan. 30 aghneghuyaghngaaghmi ayuquq nakmikillghem.

Amalleqa wataghllak state-em igleghallghem license-eghraghangluten nalighmeng state-em ID card-anganeng nakmikillghem sivungani, taanangukan atuqnaqegken nakimikistem ID-a. Seghaghhu natelnguut nakmikistemun ID-kaghqat state-egpeni.

Ilangi state-eni kiyaghsaghqaniisi 30 aghneq ilatfagilgavek nakmikimun. Pilleghqiighutkuvek ilatimun state-eghraghaghpeni tuungliin nakmikivigem sivungani, esghaghaghhnaken state-elugpek nakmikivigan qepghaghviga.

Nuugtengngukuvek 30 aghneghem ukavantellghani umiilegpagem nakmikilleghpiganeng taam ilatesneghitngukangaten, state-ekuvinghaghpek nakmikisaghqaqaten gginasaghlluten nalighmeng aviilluten nakmikisighqun.

Kingunganeng ilaasaghqaguten enkaam nakmikiyaghqaaghsaghqaguten state-eghraghaghpeni.

Enkaam wataghllak elqughaghngalghiimeng ID-lguyaghqaten state-eghraghaghpeni, tawani nakmikiyuwhaaghnaqngukuvek. Esghaqaghluki natelngughmeng ID-lguyaghqaten state-egpeni atuqsaghqan.

Nakmikivigen

Nakmikinaqniluten piiqaaten nakmikivigmi apellghitni. Nuugtekuvek, nutaghtekuvgu nakmikilleghpek ilatellgha negpek qepghaghtengitnun tuunaqaten nutaghaghmeng nakmikivigmeng neghraghaghpeni.
